Nick Thompson

Digital Design · Front-End Development · Web Build

I'm Nick Thompson, a Lead Digital Designer and Front-End Developer based in the UK, available for remote work worldwide.
I've spent over a decade helping brands make their digital presence actually work: visually sharp, user-friendly, and built to perform. My five years leading the design team for campaigns at Asda saw me designing and coding bespoke experiences for household names like P&G, LEGO, Coca-Cola, and Cadbury, taking projects from wireframe to live build across a constant stream of commercial deadlines.
I care about the craft on both sides. Good design should look and feel right, but it should also function flawlessly, and I have the development skills to make sure it does.
Currently open to senior and lead roles at in-house brands and agencies. If you've got an interesting brief or an opportunity worth talking about, I'd love to hear from you.
