Website Design & Build

Back to portfolio

Throughout my career in both freelance and agency roles, I've designed and built a wide range of websites, specialising in WordPress since 2014. My work spans everything from e-commerce sites to more complex, content-heavy builds, always tailored to the needs of each client and their audience.


Technical note: All of the projects featured here were built in WordPress using a lightweight starter theme, then fully customised with bespoke designs for each brand.


Below is a selection of websites I have designed and developed entirely from scratch.

Tigon Scaffolding

RedesignWordPressMotion

I redesigned and rebuilt the Tigon Scaffolding website after their original site had gradually become cluttered and unfocused. Stronger typography, imagery, and subtle motion help bring personality to the brand — the site now feels cohesive, professional, and better aligned with how the business operates today.

JL Dental Care

HealthcareWordPressBranding

A clean, calm, and professional website that feels welcoming rather than clinical. I refined the logo and adjusted the colour palette to create a reassuring user experience — helping patients quickly find information about treatments and booking.

LM Associates

ArchitectureWordPressPortfolio

A bespoke, minimal, image-led website for an architecture firm that puts their work front and centre. Clean typography and thoughtful spacing give their projects space to breathe while maintaining a strong, cohesive professional presence.

Dynamic Sameday Logistics

LogisticsWordPressHealthcare

A clear, professional website for a healthcare logistics company — structured layout with strong visuals and direct messaging that builds trust, communicates reliability, and helps users understand their services at a glance.

The Swim Team

Brand IdentityWordPressInteractive

Built from scratch with no existing brand guidelines — I collaborated closely with the client to establish a full brand identity alongside the site. Vibrant design, interactive maps, clear pricing, and simple booking combine to create a welcoming experience for families finding swimming lessons.
