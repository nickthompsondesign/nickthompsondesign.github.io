Website Design & Build

Throughout my career in both freelance and agency roles, I’ve designed and built a wide range of websites, specialising in WordPress since 2014. My work spans everything from e-commerce sites to more complex, content-heavy builds, always tailored to the needs of each client and their audience.


Technical note: All of the projects featured here were built in WordPress using a lightweight starter theme, then fully customised with bespoke designs for each brand.


Below is a selection of websites I have designed and developed entirely from scratch.


Tigon Scaffolding




I redesigned and rebuilt the Tigon Scaffolding website after their original site had gradually become cluttered and unfocused. As they added more content, the structure had lost its original purpose, so the aim was to bring clarity back while giving the brand a more contemporary feel.

The new design simplifies the layout, improves navigation, and presents their services more clearly, while also introducing more character and visual interest. Stronger typography, imagery, and subtle motion help bring personality to the brand and make the subject feel more engaging. Overall, the site feels more cohesive, professional, and better aligned with how the business operates today.


JL Dental Care




I designed and built a clean, calm, and professional website for JL Dental Care that feels welcoming rather than clinical. They wanted something that would work alongside their existing marketing materials, so I made small refinements to the logo and adjusted their colour palette, toning down shades that had previously felt too saturated.

The layout is simple and easy to navigate, helping patients quickly find information about treatments and booking. The design uses softer tones, clear typography, and well-structured content to create a reassuring user experience, resulting in a modern, approachable website that supports the practice’s reputation.


LM Associates




I designed a bespoke website for LM Associates that puts their architectural work front and centre. The layout is minimal and image-led, giving their projects space to breathe while keeping the site clear and easy to navigate.

The site works both as a portfolio and a professional presence, using clean typography and thoughtful spacing to complement their work. The overall design reflects their design-led approach and presents their projects in a strong, cohesive way.


Dynamic Sameday Logistics




I designed and built a clear, professional website for Dynamic Sameday Logistics that reflects the importance of their work in healthcare logistics. The design balances a modern look with straightforward information, so users can quickly understand their services.

The layout is structured and easy to navigate, with strong visuals and clear messaging to build trust and credibility. The final site feels reliable, efficient, and appropriate for a company working within critical healthcare delivery.


The Swim Team




I partnered with The Swim Team to create a brand-new website from scratch, balancing a polished, professional look with a playful, kid-friendly aesthetic. With no existing brand guidelines, I collaborated closely with them to establish a cohesive brand identity.

The website features a vibrant design and easy navigation, prioritizing accessibility and user experience. Key elements include interactive maps, clear pricing, and simple booking options. Playful visuals and child-focused imagery create a welcoming atmosphere, while testimonials build community trust.

Overall, the site provides quick access to essential information about lessons, locations, and services, connecting families with safe and enjoyable swimming lessons.
