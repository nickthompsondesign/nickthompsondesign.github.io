Lewis Thompson — Concert Visuals

Animation · Motion · Live Performance

I animated the original artwork from the artist's latest singles, transforming them into visually engaging loops with a strong focus on highlighting the artist's name. Variations of the original artwork were created to enhance visual appeal across different songs.

These animations were showcased during Lewis's live performance at the Life is Beautiful festival in Las Vegas — a three-day event that draws over 170,000 attendees.
