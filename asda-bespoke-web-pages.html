Asda Bespoke Web Pages

Bespoke campaign pages and branded zones for Asda.com — from experiential hubs with live competitions to fully custom supplier brand pages.

The Brief

Designing and building bespoke web pages for Asda's grocery site, working closely with major FMCG suppliers across Experiential Hubs and Branded Zones.

My Role

End-to-end: concept, wireframing, UX design, front-end build, supplier collaboration, and design consultancy.

Experiential Hubs

Web · UX · Campaign

The Experiential Hubs were event-focused campaign pages designed to complement in-store activations — from the Big Night In and Lunar New Year to A Magical Christmas. Rather than simply replicating in-store activity, they created a distinct online experience with instant-win competitions and supplier brand showcases.

I was responsible for shaping the vision for this product from scratch: wireframing, prototyping, refining the UX, and leading development of the first Hub. I also built the instant-win competition mechanism — an interactive iFrame integrated into the Asda Groceries site.

Process & Approach

I identified the need for an engaging page that required minimal supplier involvement — suppliers simply nominated products and provided logos. From there I sketched wireframes, built prototypes, and iterated through user testing to ensure intuitive navigation.

Results

The success of the first Hub turned Experiential Hubs into a standalone product aligned with major Asda events. The Lunar New Year campaign received over 1.1 million competition entries — a 25% increase from the previous campaign — while the Christmas edition drove 57,000 entries and nearly 1 million clicks from the Rewards App.

Branded Zones

Web · Branding · Design Consultancy

Asda offers two tiers of bespoke pages to suppliers: Simple and Complex Branded Zones. When suppliers had limited design resources, I created fully bespoke designs on their behalf — collaborating closely to understand each brand's identity, goals, and audience.

Design Consultancy

In addition to building pages, I acted as a design consultant for leading brands — creating wireframes, developing customisable templates, and guiding suppliers through the process of building brand zones that met Asda's standards while achieving their own brand goals.
