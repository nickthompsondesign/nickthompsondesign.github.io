Saro Sushi Mobile App & Branding



This project focused on the Masaco visual identity, a concept for a food brand specializing in masa harina and coconut oil-based snacks. Offering a flavorful yet nourishing experience, Masaco’s products, like tortilla chips, combine authentic Mexican flavors with a modern, healthy twist.


The branding was inspired by the sun, sea, and coconuts, with design elements rooted in tribal Mayan culture. I developed a visual identity that blends tradition with modernity, using vibrant colors, bold typography, and packaging to reflect the brand’s commitment to authenticity, quality, and healthy living.

Value proposition


1. Describe your product's features and benefits. The app offers a customisable sushi ordering experience, where users can modify ingredients, remove allergens, and adjust portion sizes. It features a well-organised menu with detailed descriptions, images, and allergen information to help users make informed decisions. Key benefits include real-time order tracking, quick reordering of favorites, and a streamlined checkout process. The app also provides price filtering, promotions, and loyalty rewards, offering a convenient and efficient experience for all users.
2. Explain the value of the product. The app’s value lies in its ability to meet the diverse needs of its users by combining customisation, speed, and reliability. It provides a tailored ordering experience with clear allergen information, easy navigation, and fast processing, which alleviates frustrations like slow checkout or inaccurate orders. The app supports users with dietary restrictions, busy schedules, or those looking for convenience, ensuring they can confidently place an order and enjoy a meal without unnecessary stress or delays.
3. Connect these features and benefits with the needs of your users. The app effectively connects with the needs of its users by addressing their core pain points. Health-conscious users benefit from the ability to customise their orders, avoid allergens, and receive clear ingredient descriptions. Users with busy schedules value the app's quick ordering, real-time tracking, and price filtering, which ensures they stay within budget while enjoying their meals on the go. Users who are less tech-savvy appreciate the simple, user-friendly design and the clear allergen information, ensuring safe and stress-free ordering, especially when ordering for groups. For all users, the app's ability to quickly reorder favorites, provide detailed ingredient breakdowns, and ensure order accuracy guarantees a seamless, efficient, and reliable experience, helping them save time and make informed choices.
