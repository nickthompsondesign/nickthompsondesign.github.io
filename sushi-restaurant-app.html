Saro Sushi Mobile App & Branding



I was tasked with developing a brand and designing a sushi menu & ordering app that enhances the experience through customisation, efficiency, and reliability. The app features an intuitive menu, real-time order tracking, and a seamless checkout process, catering to diverse user needs from busy professionals to health-conscious diners.


Brand Development


For Sora Sushi, I crafted a clean, modern brand identity that blends the elegance of Japanese cuisine with user-friendly technology. A minimalist colour palette inspired by fresh sushi ingredients—deep blues, greens, and warm reds—creates a sense of authenticity and freshness. The sleek typography and simple yet memorable logo reinforce both quality and ease of use.


The brand’s tone and messaging focus on personalisation, efficiency, and trust. Warm, clear language reassures users, especially those with dietary concerns, while a seamless user experience makes ordering stress-free. By combining strong visuals with a customer-first approach, Sora Sushi delivers a premium yet approachable ordering experience.








Understanding Users & Identifying Pain Points


Through user research, I identified three key personas: Sophie, a designer with dietary restrictions needing quick customisation; Raj, a sales executive prioritising speed and price filtering; and Linda, a retired teacher who prefers a simple, user-friendly app. Insights showed users value customisation, speed, and ease of navigation, often using filters to find items and double-check orders. They feel frustrated with slow apps, anxious about order accuracy, and relieved when reordering is easy.


Research & Defining the Problem


Based on my research, I defined the problem as the need for an easy-to-navigate sushi app that allows users to browse the menu, customise orders, and track deliveries in real time. The app focuses on customisation, speed, efficiency, transparency, and simple navigation to ensure a smooth, stress-free experience for all users, including those less tech-savvy.

Problem Statements - Understanding User's Pain Points


Sophie Taylor is a senior graphic designer who needs a sushi ordering app that allows for fast customisation and provides clear allergen information because she has dietary restrictions and values efficiency during her busy workday.


Raj Patel is a sales executive with a hectic schedule who needs a sushi app that allows for quick ordering and real-time tracking because he values speed, convenience, and staying on budget while managing his fast-paced lifestyle.


Linda Adams is a retired school teacher who needs a simple, user-friendly sushi app that provides clear allergen information and meal sharing options because she wants an easy, stress-free ordering experience with confidence in the safety and accuracy of her choices.


Ideation & Feature Development


In the ideation phase, I focused on features that addressed user pain points, such as order customisation, allergen removal, and portion adjustments. The app includes a visually rich menu with high-quality images, detailed descriptions, and allergen information. Features like quick reordering, real-time order tracking, smart filtering by price and preferences, and a loyalty rewards system enhance the user experience. I structured the app's information architecture with a clear, intuitive flow, ensuring easy navigation from featured deals to checkout.

Wireframing & Prototypes


Using Figma, I first developed low-fidelity wireframes to refine the layout and usability. Through usability testing, I improved button placement for better accessibility, adjusted font sizes and contrast for readability, and streamlined the checkout process to enhance efficiency. The final high-fidelity prototype featured a Japanese-inspired aesthetic with a clean, modern UI, soft colours, and elegant typography. The user experience was designed for seamless navigation, incorporating an interactive menu, smooth transitions, and real-time order tracking to reduce wait-time anxiety. Multiple payment options, including Apple Pay and Google Pay, ensured a convenient and flexible checkout process.

Sora Sushi successfully bridges the gap between speed, customisation, and reliability in a sushi ordering app. By focusing on user needs and pain points, I designed a seamless, intuitive, and visually engaging experience that makes ordering sushi effortless.

Value proposition


The app offers a customisable sushi ordering experience, where users can modify ingredients, remove allergens, and adjust portion sizes. It features a well-organised menu with detailed descriptions, images, and allergen information to help users make informed decisions. Key benefits include real-time order tracking, quick reordering of favorites, and a streamlined checkout process. The app also provides price filtering, promotions, and loyalty rewards, offering a convenient and efficient experience for all users.


The app’s value lies in its ability to meet the diverse needs of its users by combining customisation, speed, and reliability. It provides a tailored ordering experience with clear allergen information, easy navigation, and fast processing, which alleviates frustrations like slow checkout or inaccurate orders. The app supports users with dietary restrictions, busy schedules, or those looking for convenience, ensuring they can confidently place an order and enjoy a meal without unnecessary stress or delays.


The app effectively connects with the needs of its users by addressing their core pain points. Health-conscious users benefit from the ability to customise their orders, avoid allergens, and receive clear ingredient descriptions. Users with busy schedules value the app's quick ordering, real-time tracking, and price filtering, which ensures they stay within budget while enjoying their meals on the go. Users who are less tech-savvy appreciate the simple, user-friendly design and the clear allergen information, ensuring safe and stress-free ordering, especially when ordering for groups. For all users, the app's ability to quickly reorder favorites, provide detailed ingredient breakdowns, and ensure order accuracy guarantees a seamless, efficient, and reliable experience, helping them save time and make informed choices.
