Various Print Projects

Here’s a showcase of the diverse print projects I’ve worked on. These projects required close collaboration with printers to ensure top print quality, meticulous attention to detail to avoid misprints, and have included large-scale runs, with some jobs exceeding 500,000 units.


Yorkshire Attractions Voucher Booklets




I was responsible for designing the seasonal Yorkshire Attractions voucher booklets, which were renewed and produced three times per year, with print runs of up to 500,000 units per edition.


Museums Sheffield Wedding Brochure





As part of a new initiative by Museums Sheffield, I was tasked with designing and implementing a wedding brochure for their event spaces. This project included developing new brand colors and styling specifically for Museums Sheffield's event venues, as well as setting up a dedicated microsite. Additionally, I managed the print production to ensure a high-quality, premium finish for the brochure.


William's Den Pizza Menu




I designed the pizza menu for William's Den, focusing on creating a fun, family-friendly layout that aligned with the venue's playful aesthetic while ensuring readability and brand consistency.



Various Awards Shows Printed Materials



I was responsible for the creation of all digital and printed assets for both the SCR Hospitality Awards and the LHVA Hospitality Awards. This included designing and producing sting videos, printed programs, posters, digital advertisements, cut-out stands, and animated videos. My work ensured a cohesive and engaging visual experience across all event materials, enhancing the overall presentation and branding of both award shows.
