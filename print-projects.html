A selection of print projects spanning large-scale production runs, premium brochures, menus, and event materials. Each required close collaboration with printers to ensure top print quality — with some jobs exceeding 500,000 units.

Yorkshire Attractions Voucher Booklets

Print · Production

I was responsible for designing the seasonal Yorkshire Attractions voucher booklets — renewed and produced three times per year, with print runs of up to 500,000 units per edition. Meticulous attention to detail was essential to avoid costly misprints at this scale.

Museums Sheffield Wedding Brochure

Print · Branding · Web

As part of a new initiative by Museums Sheffield, I designed a wedding brochure for their event spaces — developing new brand colours and styling specifically for their venues, setting up a dedicated microsite, and managing print production for a premium final finish.

William's Den Pizza Menu

Print · Brand

I designed the pizza menu for William's Den, creating a fun, family-friendly layout that aligned with the venue's playful aesthetic while maintaining brand consistency and readability for all ages.

Awards Show Materials

Print · Events · Branding

I was responsible for the creation of all digital and printed assets for both the SCR Hospitality Awards and the LHVA Hospitality Awards — including sting videos, printed programmes, posters, digital advertisements, cut-out stands, and animated videos. My work ensured a cohesive and engaging visual experience across all event materials.
